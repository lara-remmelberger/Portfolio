Media Bias: The Weight of Your Trust

How much does the medium influence our trust? This interactive installation features a physical scale that tilts based on visitor votes, visualizing collective skepticism toward AI. By showcasing a timeline of historical media critiques, it prompts visitors to re-evaluate their own biases.

My Role: Concept, Hardware Programming (MicroPython/Raspberry Pi Pico), Interaction Design, and Video Production. Fabrication in collaboration with N. Menzl.
